ABOUT THE MW APARTMENT

MW Apartments has been thoughtfully and lovingly refurbished and proudly offers a pristine, stylish and comfortable accommodation while boasting magnificent, uninterrupted harbour and sea views. The Apartment comprises of a bright and welcoming hallway which allows access to all of the rooms, coat hooks and a place to leave your shoes / boots. A superb open plan room benefits from a lounge, dining area and a fully equipped kitchen with integrated appliances. This spacious room is bright and airy and provides the amazing views from the dual aspect shuttered windows. The stunning main bedroom occupies a luxurious double bed, beside cabinets with lamps, dressing table, wardrobe and the all important full length mirror. The dual aspect windows are fitted with chic black out blinds. Bedroom two is a charming twin room which features two comfortable single beds, bedside cabinets with lamps and a wardrobe with a full length mirror. The window is also fitted with a chic black out blind. The modern bathroom comprises of a bath with an overhead shower, hand basin and a WC. Outside: Seaesta’s elevated garden ” The Crow’s Nest ” has been beautifully landscaped to provide a delightful place to sit and relax while looking out to sea over Seaesta’s rooftop.

Whisky and castle trails can be enjoyed nearby.
